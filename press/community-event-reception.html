Community Reception

On 28 February 2026, the Office of Zak Farnworth hosted a reception to celebrate local community leaders and their contributions.

The event brought together individuals and organisations who have made a meaningful impact in their communities. It provided an opportunity to recognise their work and to strengthen connections for future collaboration.

Photographs and further details from the reception will be made available on this website in the coming days.
